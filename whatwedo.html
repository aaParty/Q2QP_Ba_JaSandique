Activities and Participation

Parenting Education Centers


These centers educate parents and caregivers with positive learning techniques for children and how to properly take care of them.

Online Safety Initiatives


Advocates proper policies and guidelines for online behavior and protection from cyberbullying, cyberthreats, hackers, innapropriate content, and etc.

Special Caregiving Services


These services specialize in caring for children and keeping them safe while their parents are currently unnavailable to keep their children entertained.

Finding Friends Center


This center help young children befriend other children by placing them in a daycare, having them interact with other children.

Community Support Networks


Establishes supportive networks provide resources, counseling, help, and advice to any people who are in need of these kinds of services.

Foster Care System


Helps ensure that children receive better proper care, treatment, support, and education with their new family.

School Programs


These programs educate children about proper safety and care about oneself and others, how to defend themselves in case of danger.

Local Orphanages


We help local children who are in need of families with food, supplies, toys, books, and etc. for personal entertainment.

Child Abuse Prevention Campaigns


We strongly detest the act of abusing children and plan to end this global problem by helping campaigns all over the world.

Policy for Child Protection


We need proper rules to set an ideal example on how to efficiently care for a child.

Mental Support Services


We care for the state of one's mind and understand that it will affect one's daily life, so we try to provide as much mental support we can.

Development Programs


We strongly recommend children to have extracurricular activities to enhance their mindset and improve their body and mind.
